Introduction

LLM Grounding Challenge @ ISWC 2025

This challenge focuses on a critical issue in large language model (LLM) deployment—controlling the sources of information used in generated responses. While LLMs exhibit fluency and reasoning capabilities, their embedded factual knowledge may be outdated or domain-inappropriate. This challenge is designed to test whether AI systems can selectively draw upon either their internal knowledge or external documents based on explicit instructions.

This challenge focuses on building AI systems that can selectively draw upon the correct source of information—either from internal model weights or from provided external documents/context—based on explicit instructions. Systems will be evaluated on how well they adhere to these directives, ensuring that facts are grounded only in the specified sources.
